What Is Katipunan SMP?

Katipunan SMP is more than a Minecraft server — it's a living world shaped entirely by its players. Born from the spirit of bayanihan and Filipino camaraderie, our community thrives on creativity, adventure, and shared history.

From building thriving towns to forging powerful alliances and nation-states, every decision you make writes the next chapter of our ever-evolving world. Forge friendships. Build empires. Create history.
